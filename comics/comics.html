Star Wars: Doctor Aphra is an ongoing comic-book series that began publication on December 7, 2016. It is written by Star Wars: Darth Vader writer Kieron Gillen with illustrations by Kev Walker.[2] The series takes place between Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope and Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, and follows from the conclusion of Star Wars: Darth Vader. It focuses on the character Doctor Chelli Lona Aphra.
